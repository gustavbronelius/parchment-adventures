## Quests

- **Status**: Early Game
- **Details**: Protect your island base from rival pirates.

### Island Exploration
- **Status**: Ongoing
- **Details**: Discover resources and secrets on the island.

### Recover the Ancestral Cutlass
- **Status**: Mid Game
- **Details**: Retrieve your lost family cutlass.
